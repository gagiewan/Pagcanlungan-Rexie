What is the Lantern Festival?

The Lantern Festival is a traditional Chinese festival held on the 15th day of the first lunar month, signaling the end of the Chinese New Year festivities. The festival is a joyous occasion marked by the lighting of colorful lanterns that symbolize the hope and good fortune for the year ahead.
